EXT. ROAD -- MORNING

A blue car drives quickly down the highway. We see a Florida state sticker on the back window as the car passes a sign that says "Welcome to Woodstock, Alabama!" A highway sign on the other side of the road reads: "Montgomery 85 mi."

Finally, a taller-than-average man steps out. This is Tyler Goodson. He's young - in his twenties - but he looks older than usual today. He's wearing a tank top and we see his arms are covered in tattoos. His face is smooth and round until it's interrupted by his curly brown beard. Tyler walks towards the front door. As he approaches the entrance, he pulls a keychain from his pocket. He steps up to the door and pauses. He closes his eyes, breathes deeply, and unlocks the door.

INT. CAR -- MORNING

We see an older couple, a Reta and Charlie Lawrence, in the car. Reta is driving and Charlie is sitting in the passenger seat. Reta's face is heavier than can be explained by the long drive; she has bags under her eyes and she's slouching forward. Charlie sits stiff and straight. He has a detached look as he gazes out of the passenger side window.

The car pulls into a parking lot. The nearest building has a sign that reads "Bibb County Sheriff's Office"
